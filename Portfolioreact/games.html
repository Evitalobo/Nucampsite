Evita is a UI/UX designer and front end developer. They graduated from UCSC with a Computer Science: Game Design(B.S.) degree. They focused on user centric design with creating games and applications that users could easily engage with. When they are not creating/ redesigning systems, they can be found painting, creating jewelry, gardening and spending time in nature.

Window Pane

Window Pane is a first person narrative puzzle game based on Project MKUltra. The player agrees to be a clinical trial test-subject but things go horribly wrong. The player must figure out how to navigate around the house and which evidence and story paths will help them successfully escape.
  • Roles: Creator, Producer, UX Designer, Game Designer, Developer
  • Languages: C#, GLSL
  • Tools: Unity 3D with Oculus VR

Generative Design

A collection of generative design projects that use p5.js . They include Music Visualization with particle systems, Terrain generation with Perlin Noise, Interactive Artist with Generative Grammars, Evolving Cars using Genetic Algorithms and Music with Markhov Models
  • Roles: Developer
  • Languages: Javascript, HTML
  • Tools: p5.js

Crystal Caves

Crystal Caves is a 4-Directional adventure game about an amnesiac scientist who wakes up on an island with traces of an extinct civilization. The player navigates the island by solving puzzles, unlocking new tools and collecting resources.
  • Roles: Creator, Developer, User Experience Designer , Game Designer , Environment Artist
  • Languages: Javascript, HTML, CSS
  • Tools: Phaser2

Wild West Wolf

An endless runner featuring a Mexican Wolf- one of the most endangered wolves in North America. Avoid the hunter traps and collect stars to boost your score.
  • Roles: Creator, Developer, Artist
  • Languages: Javascript, HTML, CSS
  • Tools: Phaser2

Kundalini

Kundalini is a term that originated in ancient India that refers to the energy that is coiled at the base of the spine, which starts at the first chakra, the root chakra. You must open all chakras- energy vortices that run along the human spine and maintain energy balance.
  • Roles: Creator, Developer, Artist
  • Tools: Phaser2

Mycology Memory Master

All mushrooms in the game can be found locally in Santa Cruz county. An educational tool for learning and identifying mushrooms in local hiking spots around the region featuring handpainted cards and a compilation of mycologic identification information.
  • Roles: Creator, Artist
  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator
